== Description ==


Syra AI Chatbot engages store visitors with human-like conversations, answers their questions contextually, and leads them to pre-defined business goals – all automated, enterprise-ready and deployed 24×7!



== Plugin Features ==

With Syra AI Chatbot, you can :

1. **Make Human-Like Conversations** Syra AI Chatbots can conduct human-like conversations where they understand the context of the questions asked and reply accordingly.
2. **Drive to Business Goals** Syra AI Chatbots handle user conversations intelligently so that they converge on specific business goals as defined by the customer.
3. **Do More while Spending Less** Syra AI Chatbots bring in the powers of Artificial Intelligence (AI) to Shopify Store Owners with a very affordable per-month pricing.
4. **Optimize Performances for Better Business Outcomes** Syra AI Chatbots provide deep, detailed & insightful analytical dashboards & reports so that customers can measure and understand how exactly are their chatbots performing. They can then fine-tune the chatbot's settings for achieving better business outcomes.

You could use these Top Features of the Plugin:

> Understands the intent and the context of the questions asked by the user. 
> Uses advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) technologies.
> Relevant responses are culled from the knowledge base as ingested & trained.
> Leads the User through a conversational funnel towards predefined business goals. 
> The funnel path is determined in real time based on the current conversations. 
> Guarantees higher sales conversions.

== Installation ==

= Automatic installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option -- WordPress will handles the file transfer, and you won’t need to leave your web browser. To auto-install Syra AI Chatbot, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu, and click “Add New.”
 
In the search field type “Syra AI Chatbot,” then click “Search Plugins.” Once you find it,  you can view details about it such as the point release, rating, and description. Most importantly, you just need to click on  “Install Now,” and WordPress will take it from there.

= Manual installation =

Manual installation method requires downloading the Syra AI Chatbot plugin and uploading it to your web server via your favorite FTP application. The WordPress codex contains [instructions on how to do this here](https://wordpress.org/support/article/managing-plugins/#manual-plugin-installation).

= Configure =

After installing and activating the plugin you will get a form to register with Syra AI Chatbot. (If you have not any account, then register to (https://chatbots.syra.ai/) and activate the account from your email).
To register fill in the form with registered email of (https://chatbots.syra.ai/) and password , click on “Save” and “Proceed to Syra”
